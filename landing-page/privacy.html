Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Introduction

Hair Studio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

  • Photos: When you use the try-on feature, you upload or capture photos. These are processed to generate hairstyle previews and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data (OS version, device model) for app compatibility and crash reporting.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website. This collects anonymized usage data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the hairstyle try-on service
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To fix bugs and prevent crashes

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Photos are processed securely and deleted after generation.

Data Security

All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated for ages 3+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: support@hairstudioapp.com
