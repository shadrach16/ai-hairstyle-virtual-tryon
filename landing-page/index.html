Frequently Asked Questions

How does Hair Studio work?

Simply upload a selfie or take a photo, browse through 100+ hairstyles, and tap to see how each style looks on you. Our AI engine maps the hairstyle to your face shape and skin tone for a realistic preview.

Is Hair Studio free to download?

Yes! Hair Studio is free to download from the Google Play Store. You get free credits to try on styles, with optional premium plans for unlimited generations.

What hairstyles are available?

We offer 100+ styles including braids, fades, locs, afros, bobs, pixie cuts, curtain bangs, long waves, cornrows, twists, weaves, and many more for all genders and hair types.

Does it work for all hair types and ethnicities?

Absolutely. Hair Studio is designed for everyone — all hair textures (straight, wavy, curly, coily), all skin tones, and all genders. Our AI adapts the hairstyle to look natural on your unique features.

What devices can run Hair Studio?

Hair Studio runs on any Android phone or tablet with Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer. It requires a camera or photo gallery access for the try-on feature.

Is my photo data safe?

Yes. Your photos are processed securely and we do not store or share your personal images. All data is encrypted in transit and you can request deletion at any time.
